Upload Guidelines

  • Supported files: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, PNG, ZIP.
  • Use a unique document number for every document.
  • Verify the selected category before saving.
  • Only upload final and approved documents.
  • Avoid special characters in filenames when possible.
  • Review all document details before submission.
